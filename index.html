⚙ Q&A

Q: what is rsbsPLUS?

A: rsbsPLUS is a series of ports with enhancements of jefe (a.k.a. JayFoxRox)'s RSBS homebrew for the V.Smile. It then became a meme and I'm here beating on a dead horse making this basically another kind of "Hello World!"

Q: Why would I choose one version over the other?

A: Well, first off, so you can play the game on whatever platform you like among the supported ones. Secondly, I am trying to spice things up by giving each port something special

For example: the GB<C> version is pretty cute imo. The DS version uses both screens with the console held like a book and seamless transition from top to bottom and vice-versa. The GBA version has a nice scrolling background... etc etc
